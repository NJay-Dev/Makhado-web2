LIST OF BIDS RECEIVED

In accordance with the Municipal Finance Management Act (MFMA) and Supply Chain Management Regulations, Makhado Local Municipality publishes the list of bids received for each tender after the closing date.

This page provides transparency in the bidding process by showing all bids that were received for each tender.

About Bids Received

The publication of bids received is part of the municipality's commitment to transparency and accountability in the procurement process.

After the closing date of a tender, the following process takes place:

Tender Opening

All bids received before the closing time are opened in public, and the names of bidders are recorded.

Recording

Details of all bids received are recorded in the municipality's tender register.

Publication

The list of bids received is published on the municipal website and notice boards.

Evaluation

Bids are then evaluated by the Bid Evaluation Committee according to the criteria specified in the tender document.
