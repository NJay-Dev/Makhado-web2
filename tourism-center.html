MAKHADO TOURISM CENTRE

Welcome to the Makhado Tourism Centre, your gateway to exploring the rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and diverse attractions of the Makhado region. Our tourism centre provides information, resources, and guidance to help visitors make the most of their stay in our beautiful municipality.

Below you can find tourism resources, accommodation listings, and development plans that showcase what Makhado has to offer.

Discover Makhado

Natural Attractions

Explore the stunning Soutpansberg Mountains, nature reserves, and scenic landscapes that make Makhado a nature lover's paradise.

Cultural Heritage

Discover the rich cultural heritage of the Venda, Tsonga, and other local communities through museums, craft markets, and cultural villages.

Adventure Activities

Experience thrilling outdoor activities including hiking, mountain biking, bird watching, and 4x4 trails throughout the region.
