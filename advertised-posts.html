CURRENT ADVERTISED POSTS

Makhado Local Municipality is committed to employment equity and diversity. We invite suitably qualified candidates to apply for the following positions. All positions are subject to the terms and conditions of the Local Government: Municipal Systems Act, as amended.

Please ensure that you submit all required documentation as specified in the job advertisements. Incomplete applications will not be considered.

How to Apply

Applications must be submitted on the official Makhado Municipality Employment Application Form, which can be downloaded below.

Required Documents

Completed application form, certified copies of qualifications, ID document, driver's license (if required), and detailed CV.

Submission

Applications can be submitted via email, post, or hand-delivered to the Municipal offices as specified in the job advertisement.

Closing Dates

Applications received after the closing date will not be considered. Please ensure timely submission.

Important Notice

Canvassing for appointment will lead to automatic disqualification. If you do not receive a response within 30 days, please consider your application unsuccessful.
