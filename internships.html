INTERNSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

Makhado Local Municipality offers internship programmes to provide graduates with practical work experience in their field of study. These internships are designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and workplace skills.

This page provides information about current internship opportunities available within the municipality.

About Our Internship Programme

Internships at Makhado Local Municipality typically run for a period of 12-24 months, providing graduates with valuable workplace experience.

Eligibility

Internships are open to recent graduates with qualifications relevant to local government operations.

Work Experience

Interns gain practical work experience in their field of study under the guidance of experienced professionals.

Mentorship

Each intern is assigned a mentor who provides guidance, support, and regular feedback throughout the internship.

Stipend

Interns receive a monthly stipend to cover basic expenses during the internship programme.
