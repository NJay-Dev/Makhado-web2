CANCELLED TENDERS

In accordance with the Municipal Finance Management Act (MFMA) and Supply Chain Management Regulations, Makhado Local Municipality publishes information about cancelled tenders to ensure transparency in the procurement process.

This page provides information about tenders that have been cancelled by the municipality for various reasons.

About Cancelled Tenders

Tenders may be cancelled for various legitimate reasons in accordance with the municipality's Supply Chain Management Policy and applicable legislation.

Common reasons for tender cancellation include:

No Acceptable Bids

When no bids meet the minimum functionality criteria or other requirements specified in the tender document.

Changed Circumstances

When the municipality's needs or requirements have changed since the tender was advertised.

Budget Constraints

When all bids received exceed the budgeted amount for the project or service.

Specification Issues

When errors or ambiguities in the tender specifications are identified after advertisement.
