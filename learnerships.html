LEARNERSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

Makhado Local Municipality is committed to skills development and empowering the youth through learnership programmes. Learnerships combine theoretical training with practical workplace experience, leading to a nationally recognized qualification.

This page provides information about current learnership opportunities available within the municipality.

About Learnerships

Learnerships are structured learning programmes that combine theoretical training with practical workplace experience.

Qualification

Learners receive a nationally recognized qualification upon successful completion of the programme.

Training

Theoretical training is provided by accredited training providers in various fields relevant to local government.

Practical Experience

Learners gain valuable workplace experience through practical assignments and on-the-job training.

Stipend

Learners receive a monthly stipend to cover basic expenses during the learnership programme.
