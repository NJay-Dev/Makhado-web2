FIXED TERM CONTRACT (EPWP) OPPORTUNITIES

The Expanded Public Works Programme (EPWP) is a nationwide programme that aims to provide poverty and income relief through temporary work for the unemployed. Makhado Local Municipality participates in this programme by offering fixed-term contract opportunities to community members.

This page provides information about current EPWP opportunities available within the municipality.

About the EPWP Programme

The EPWP is one element of a broader government strategy to reduce poverty through the alleviation and reduction of unemployment.

Job Creation

The EPWP creates temporary work opportunities for the unemployed, using public sector expenditure to alleviate poverty.

Skills Development

Participants receive training to enhance their capacity to earn an income after their participation in the programme.

Infrastructure Development

The programme contributes to the development and maintenance of public infrastructure in communities.

Community Services

EPWP workers provide essential services to communities, including cleaning, maintenance, and social care.
