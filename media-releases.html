Municipality Announces New Water Conservation Measures

April 5, 2024 | Office of the Municipal Manager

The Makhado Local Municipality has announced new water conservation measures in response to the ongoing drought conditions affecting the region. The measures include water restrictions, increased tariffs for high consumption, and a public awareness campaign.

February 15, 2024 | Finance Department

The Makhado Local Municipality has launched a new online payment system to make it easier for residents to pay their municipal bills. The system allows for payments via credit card, debit card, and electronic funds transfer, and is accessible 24/7.

Municipality Secures Funding for Major Infrastructure Projects

December 10, 2023 | Office of the Mayor

The Makhado Local Municipality has secured R150 million in funding from the National Treasury for major infrastructure projects. The funding will be used for road upgrades, water infrastructure, and electricity network improvements in various parts of the municipality.
