CURRENT ADVERTISED TENDERS

The Makhado Local Municipality is committed to fair, transparent, and competitive procurement processes. This page provides information about current tender opportunities available for interested service providers.

Interested service providers are encouraged to regularly check this page for new opportunities to do business with the municipality.

How to Apply for Tenders

All tender documents can be obtained from the Supply Chain Management Office at the Civic Centre, 83 Krogh Street, Louis Trichardt during office hours (07:45 - 16:30).

Document Collection

Visit the Supply Chain Management Office to collect tender documents. A non-refundable fee may be applicable for some tenders.

Briefing Sessions

Attend any compulsory briefing sessions as specified in the tender document. These sessions provide important information about the tender requirements.

Submission

Submit completed tender documents in sealed envelopes to the tender box at the Civic Centre before the closing date and time.

Late Submissions

Late submissions will not be accepted. Ensure that your bid is submitted before the closing time specified in the tender document.
