AWARDED TENDERS

In accordance with the Municipal Finance Management Act (MFMA) and Supply Chain Management Regulations, Makhado Local Municipality publishes information about awarded tenders to ensure transparency in the procurement process.

This page provides historical records of tenders that have been awarded by the municipality, organized by financial year.

About Awarded Tenders

The tender registers below contain information about successful bidders, including the bid number, description, awarded amount, and B-BBEE status level.

After the evaluation and adjudication process, tenders are awarded to successful bidders based on a combination of factors, including:

Functionality

The bidder's ability to deliver the required goods or services according to specifications.

Price

The competitiveness of the bid price in relation to the estimated cost and other bids received.

B-BBEE Status

The bidder's Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment status level of contribution.

Compliance

Compliance with all mandatory requirements specified in the tender document.
