Know Us!

Makhado Municipality was first established on the 31st October 1934 as the Louis Trichardt Town Council. With the new municipal demarcations, a number of municipalities were established in 1997. However, following the Municipal Structures Act of 1998, the municipalities were merged into a NP 344 Municipality that is now known as the Makhado Municipality.
